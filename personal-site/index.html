Welcome to my personal website!

Denislava Velkova is a Graphic Designer. She was originally born in Bulgaria, Sofia on June 12th, 1998 which is located in Europe next to Greece, Italy, and Serbia. She came to the United States when she was sixteen years old and graduated high school in Jacksonville Florida where she lived for two years. She is currently attending Graceland University in an effort to obtain her bachelor’s degree in Arts and Graphic Design. Her family is the most important part of her life as well as her success. Her life so far is comprised of a wonderful family, great friends, college experience, and a wonderful job where she can focus more on her projects and becoming a better Graphic Designer.
